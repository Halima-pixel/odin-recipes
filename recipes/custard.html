Custard

Description

This custard recipe is a combination of a few different recipes i have tried. It is not
too rich or too sweet as many egg custards can be. Can be served warm immediately or cold

Ingredients

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

Directions

Step 1

Place milk, vanilla extract, and butter in a saucepan. Cook and stir over medium heat
until simmering; remove from heat before it comes to a boil.

Step 3

Set the saucepan back over low heat. Pout in egg mixture slowly, whisking constantly
until custard thicken enough to coat the bottom of a spoon, 5 to 10 minutes.

Step 4

Serve warm or transfer custard into a large bowl, cover, and allow to cook before chilling.
